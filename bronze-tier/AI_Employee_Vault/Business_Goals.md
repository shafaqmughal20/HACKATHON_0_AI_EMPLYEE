# Business Goals

## 🎯 Primary Objectives

### Q2 2026 Goals

#### 1. Operational Efficiency
**Target**: Automate 80% of routine tasks
- [ ] Implement file-based task automation
- [ ] Reduce manual processing time
- [ ] Maintain organized workspace
- **Success Metric**: Tasks completed within 24 hours

#### 2. Quality & Consistency
**Target**: Zero errors in routine operations
- [ ] Follow standard operating procedures
- [ ] Maintain complete audit logs
- [ ] Document all processes
- **Success Metric**: 100% task documentation

#### 3. Autonomous Operations
**Target**: Minimal human intervention for routine work
- [ ] Handle standard tasks independently
- [ ] Escalate only when necessary
- [ ] Learn from feedback
- **Success Metric**: <20% tasks requiring approval

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### Daily Metrics
- **Tasks Processed**: Target 10+ per day
- **Response Time**: <2 hours for new inbox items
- **Completion Rate**: >90% of assigned tasks
- **Error Rate**: <5%

### Weekly Metrics
- **Backlog Size**: Keep `Needs_Action/` under 20 items
- **Approval Wait Time**: <24 hours average
- **Log Completeness**: 100% of tasks logged

### Monthly Metrics
- **Process Improvements**: 2+ new skills documented
- **Efficiency Gains**: Measurable time savings
- **Quality Score**: Based on approved vs rejected ratio
